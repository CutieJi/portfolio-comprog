Origin of Internet?

Internet has evolved through the need of advance technology in communication during the Cold War. The Department of Defense of the United States funded ARPA to research on network technologies that would continuously transmit messages or information to its final destination unless any part of the network was destroyed. This results to easy communication in military operation.

Why is Internet so Popular?

It offers massive information. You can get information for free or for a fee. You can go to a specific place without traveling and spending money to reach it. You can visit or even tour to different libraries all over the World or you can just get the information written by the experts by simply downloading it.

The tools or the facilities for communications are the major reasons why Internet became popular. One example is e-mail. This tool has made a big impact on communication. You can send messages or talk to a distant person over a second at minimal cost, unlike with long distance calls, which cost you hundreds or thousands of your money.
